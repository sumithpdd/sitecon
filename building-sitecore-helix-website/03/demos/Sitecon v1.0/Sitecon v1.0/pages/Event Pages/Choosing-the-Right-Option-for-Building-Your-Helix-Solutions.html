Choosing the Right Option for Building Your Helix Solutions

[March 25 - by Shelley Benhoff]

This session will include a discussion comparing and contrasting methods you can use to create Sitecore Helix Visual Studio projects.

First, I will review how to create Helix projects in Visual Studio from scratch and highlight the repetitive steps involved such as adding references to the Sitecore dlls, removing unnecessary folders and files, and updating properties for individual files.

Then, I will talk about Yeoman and how it works to automate much of the repetitive steps involved in creating a Helix project using PowerShell scripts.

Finally, I will look at the Sitecore Helix Templates Visual Studio Extension and how it enables developers to easily create Helix Solutions from right within the Visual Studio interface.

Shelley Benhoff

Shelley is a Sitecore MVP and Pluralsight Author. She previously worked at Sitecore as the Education Development Manager, overseeing the production of official Sitecore Training and Certification Exams. She is currently bringing her Sitecore expertise to Pluralsight where she is producing up a new Unofficial Sitecore Training curriculum.
