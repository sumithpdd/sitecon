Helix and Sitecore JavaScript Services (JSS)

[March 26 - by Jean-François L'Heureux]

JavaScript Services (JSS) is a completely new way of developing your digital experience on Sitecore. In this session, Sitecore Technology MVP Jeff L'Heureux will demonstrate how to apply the Sitecore Helix principles to the development of new sites using JSS to keep them and their project architectures clean, maintainable, and flexible.

Best Practices

Remember to keep your project architecture:

  • Clean
  • Maintainable
  • Flexible
Jean-François L'Heureux

Jean-François (Jeff) L'Heureux is a senior software developer in the demo team at Sitecore. With his front-end passion, 11 years of software development experience, and 5 years of Sitecore experience, he builds demos using the latest Sitecore technologies including JSS, SXA, and Commerce. He obtained the Sitecore Technology MVP award in 2017, 2018, and 2019 for his implication in the Sitecore community.
